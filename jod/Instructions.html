This part of the experiment involves two types of participants: Participant A and Participant B.

Participant A and Participant are each given {{ Constants.endowment|cu }}. Participant A decides whether to reduce (part of) Participant B's earnings or to leave it as it is. It costs Participant A 1 point to reduce Participant B's earnings by {{ Constants.destruction_strength|cu }}. More specifically, for each 1 point Participant A spends, Participant B's earnings will be reduced by {{ Constants.destruction_strength|cu }}. Participant A can therefore choose a number of points between 0 and 25 to spend on reducing Participant B's earnings. Participant B makes no decisions.

Final earnings are calculated as follows:

  • Participant A earns: {{ Constants.endowment|cu }} - the number of points Participant A spent on reducing Participant B's earnings
  • Participant B earns: {{ Constants.endowment|cu }} - {{ Constants.destruction_strength }} times the number of points Participant A spent on reducing Participant B's earnings
